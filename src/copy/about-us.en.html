Our Vision

Our mission is to create a system that we can access to a product, a service, and information that we want in one second.

We were told it's not possible. We’re glad we didn’t listen...

Pocketle was born with support and love from a lot of people. At the same time, there were people who told us we can't accomplish what we want. We also were crashed by others' pressure but we continued to convince to them without being overwhelmed by such their words and actions. As a result? Their attitude has changed and now they also support our goals.
We are changing that by building the world’s first and only ‘purpose search engine in the cloud’: a dream search engine filled with so much hopes. Imagine the moment that you find the products, the service and the information that you wanted in a secound but settled for the-advertised-useless-products or spent days-for-seeking-information instead.

We want to expand the possibilities of people.

We are asked what makes Pocketle different from other IT companies? We say that we are very different from them. Because they force the possibility of IT without really thinking through what IT may impact the human liveswhile pocketle's stance is that people do what they want to do. We strongly believe that it's not IT's job to take away people's work, IT should create jobs to expand people's potential. We are confident that our attitude toward trying to solve what people can not do with IT should be felt from our services and products...

We hope Pocketle can help you get closer to what you want to achieve.

Sayuri Honda
Founder, Pocketle
